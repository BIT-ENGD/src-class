Long format has 3 columns: country, key, value
  - M*N rows.
  e.g. 
  'USA', 'President', 'Obama'
   ...
  'USA', 'Currency', 'Dollar'

Wide format has N=16 columns: county, key1, ..., keyN
  - M rows
example: 
   country, President, ... , Currency
   'USA', 'Obama', ... , 'Dollar'
