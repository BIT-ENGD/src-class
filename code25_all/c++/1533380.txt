x' = x*cos(t) - y*sin(t) 
y' = x*sin(t) + y*cos(t)

where (x, y) are the original points,
(x', y') are the rotated coordinates,
and t is the angle measured in radians

from the x-axis. The rotation is
counter-clockwise as written.
