GAME DESIGN DOCUMENT
Game Name: It's Raining Kids REMIX
Designed by: Ivan Boyko
Programmed in: Swift (iOS)
Synopsis: From the developer of the original It's Raining Kids comes a new take on an old favourite. Too many kids have been saved, and they're threatening to overflow the score variable. Kids fly out of a singularity in the centre of the screen, tap on them to "save" them. Features multiple game modes and replayability.
Controls: Tap buttons to navigate through the menus. Tap kids to "save" them. After the game is over, tap to return to the menu scene.
Game Modes:
Kid Crusher - "Save" as many kids as you can in 60 seconds
Endless Mode - "Save" all the kids you can! Game ends when you miss 3 kids.
