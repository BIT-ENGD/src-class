Experiments with Nucleo32 STM32L432KC

nucleo-l432kc
Requirements

ubuntu 18.10
cmake
stm32cubel4 from STMicroelectronics (en.stm32cubel4.zip)
toolchain
openocd (to be able to flash)

Build instructions
After editing configure-cmake
bash
mkdir build
cd build
../configure-cmake ..
make
Flash an example into the board
bash
make flash_stm32-example-gpio-toggle
