Armageddon Plugins
MUSHclient plugins designed for Armageddon MUD.
Generic Installation Instructions

Download the desired plugin.
Open MUSHclient.
Navigate to File / Plugins.
Click the "Add" button.
Navigate to the downloaded file and click Open.
Close the "Plugins" menu and proceed to play.

About each Plugin
Arma_Infobar.xml
Displays your character's vital stats in a bar below your command line.
You must type InstallInfobar once you are logged into your character to setup your prompt.
Arma_Highlights.xml
Minimal highlights for keeping track during busy combat scenes.
legacy-infobar-script.lua
Has been replaced by Arma_Infobar.xml.
Originally by Delirium.
Converted in June 2007 to Lua and upgraded over the years by Robert Pate.
AnArmBarByVenomz.lua
Work in progress based on legacy-infobar-script.lua.
