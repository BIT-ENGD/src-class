The central class: Schedule
You should be able to do everything you want to do using a Schedule, since it includes members of the classes listed here that provide access to all of your data.
Parsed versions of your source data

the TopicGroup class represents the raw data in your topics file. It can cluster individual topics into groups by Week.
the CalendarConfig class represents the raw data in your calendrical configuration file.
