How to configure Facebook Login

Navigate and Login to https://developers.facebook.com/apps
Click "Add New App"
Enter your app name and contact email
In App Dashboard, click Set Up in Facebook Login section
Chose "Web" as your Platform
Set Site URL to http://localhost:3000/ for local testing. (Port of your Node server, not the port of the BrowserSync proxy)
Click Facebook Login on the left panel
Turn on Client OAuth Login and Web OAuth Login. Enter http://localhost:3000/login/facebook/return for Valid OAuth redirect URIs.
Get your App ID and Secret and copy it to src/config.js
