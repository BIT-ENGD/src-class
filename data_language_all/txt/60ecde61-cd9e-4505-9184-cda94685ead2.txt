hubot-Rabbot
A robotic helper for @coderabbi and his IRC channel
See src/rabbot.coffee for full documentation.
Installation
In hubot project repo, run:
npm install hubot-rabbot --save
Then add hubot-rabbot to your external-scripts.json:
json
[
  "hubot-rabbot"
]
Sample Interaction
user1>> hubot hello
hubot>> hello!
