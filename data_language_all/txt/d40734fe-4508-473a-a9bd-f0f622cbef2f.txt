helm-lobsters.el

Introduction
helm-lobsters.el is an helm front-end for lobste.rs.
This is an adaptation of helm-hackernews.el by Syohei YOSHIDA for lobste.rs.
If you're an ivy user check ivy-lobsters.
Screenshot

Requirements

Emacs 24 or higher
helm 1.0 or higher

Usage
m-x helm-lobsters RET shows stories with helm.
