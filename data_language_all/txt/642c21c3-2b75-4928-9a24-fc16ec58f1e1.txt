atom-memolist
show files of simply memo on atom editor.
:satisfied: I use for vim with glidenote/memolist.vim
Usage

show lists of memos ctrl+alt+m and enter keyword to grep title.

Note

:astonished: for create new memo with: atom-memolist-new
:astonished: for grep memo with: atom-memolist-grep

Link
for Sublime Text: Sublime-memolist
Setting
ctrl + shift + p and config
or edit ~/.atom/config.cson
