ESPCKML
Bookmarklet to overlay a KML file on the Edinburgh Solicitors Property Centre's maps.
Usage

Edit espckml.coffee to update the KML_URL and DESTINATIONS to those of your choice.
Compile with http://johtso.github.io/CoffeeMarklet/ into a bookmarklet.
Go to any ESPC search or property page and run the bookmarklet.
