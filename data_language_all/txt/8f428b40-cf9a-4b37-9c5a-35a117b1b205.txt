MailCount (MC)
MailCount is an ubersicht plugin to display the number of unread emails in your gmail account.
Installation

Download and move mc.widget to your ubersicht widgets folder.
Replace <user> on line 1 with your gmail account username.[^1]
Optionally, adjust the color thresholds to your liking.
