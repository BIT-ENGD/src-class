helm-rg
Helm frontend for rg (ripgrep).
Quickstart
emacs-lisp
(add-to-list 'load-path "path/to/helm-rg/")
(require 'helm-rg)

helm-rg
helm-rg-at-point
helm-projectile-rg
helm-projectile-rg-at-point
